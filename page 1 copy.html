Virat Kohli withdraws from first two Tests against England due to personal reasons

Virat Kohli will miss the first two Tests against England citing personal reasons and the replacement will be announced soon, the BCCI has announced on Monday. “Virat Kohli has requested the BCCI to be withdrawn from the first two Tests of the upcoming Test series against England, citing personal reasons,” BCCI said in a statement. In the release, BCCI has said that the former India captain has spoken to the captain, team management and selectors. “Virat has spoken to Captain Rohit Sharma, the team management and the selectors and has emphasized that while representing the country has always been his top priority, certain personal situations demand his presence and undivided attention,” read the statement.
